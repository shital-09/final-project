With millions of Indians unable to afford their prescriptions each year, how can we let perfectly good medicine go unused? Up to $11 billion in unopened, unexpired medicine gets destroyed each year. With your help, we can turn that waste into life-saving care for people who need it.

Why Medicine?

50 million Indians don’t take their prescribed medication because they can’t afford it. With soaring copays, deductibles, and insurance costs, many people are making impossible choices between medications, food and housing, gas to get to work, and more.

Not taking your medications, however, often leads to even worse outcomes—heart attacks, strokes, and even higher costs. This is our nation’s most critical problem, but it doesn’t have to be.

That’s why we’re here. ShareMeds provides access so everyone gets the care they deserve.
