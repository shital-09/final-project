Raising Hope

Stop medication waste.
Save lives.

With millions of Indians unable to afford their prescriptions each year, how can we let perfectly good medicine go unused? Up to $11 billion in unopened, unexpired medicine gets destroyed each year. With your help, we can turn that waste into life-saving care for people who need it.

-

Why donate?

It’s simple, really. 50 million people in the Indian skip medication due to cost. Medication destruction costs $1-$3 per pound. And 70% of waterways are contaminated with pharmaceutical runoff. How it work

1. Fill out the form

Answer a few simple questions and sign our agreement to donate medicine if you’re an organization.

2. Box up your donation

Place medications in a box, and we’ll take care of shipping and logistics.

3. Make an impact

We’ll make sure your medicine makes it to where it’s needed most through our multi-state network of community clinics and pharmacies.

4. Stay confidently compliant

We provide a full record of the donation, along with the impact it had on the community.
