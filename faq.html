What if my facility type is not listed?

Can individuals donate? What if I have extra medicine in my medicine cabinet?

Yes, individuals in most states can donate medicine via ShareMeds. We do not currently accept donations from individuals in Dabha Nagpur

Is donating medicine free for organizations?

Donating unopened, unexpired, non-controlled, and high-demand medicine is free for organizations; ShareMeds covers the cost of shipping. If you would like to donate all your unexpired, unopened, non-controlled medication without sorting (like single-stream recycling), you can pay a small fee to help cover costs.
